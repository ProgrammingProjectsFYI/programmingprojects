Augmented reality overlays digital content onto the real world through a camera feed. Building an AR app teaches you about computer vision, 3D transformations, and real-time rendering.

<!--more-->

You'll work with marker detection, coordinate systems, and rendering pipelines to place virtual objects convincingly into a live camera view, bridging the gap between the physical and digital worlds.
