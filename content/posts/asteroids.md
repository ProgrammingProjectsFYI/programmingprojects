Asteroids is a classic arcade game released by Atari in 1979. The player controls a spaceship in an asteroid field, shooting and avoiding asteroids.

<!--more-->

Building Asteroids involves implementing 2D physics, collision detection, vector graphics, and game state management. It's a fantastic introduction to game development fundamentals.

[Build your own Asteroids](https://codingchallenges.fyi/challenges/challenge-asteroids) is available with a project breakdown on [Coding Challenges](https://codingchallenges.fyi/).
