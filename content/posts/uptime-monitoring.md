An uptime monitoring service periodically checks whether websites and services are available, alerting when they go down.

<!--more-->

This project involves HTTP health checks, scheduling, alerting mechanisms, and dashboard reporting. It's a great way to learn about networking, reliability engineering, and building robust background services.

[Build your own Uptime Monitoring Service](https://codingchallenges.fyi/challenges/challenge-uptime-monitoring) is available with a project breakdown on [Coding Challenges](https://codingchallenges.fyi/).
